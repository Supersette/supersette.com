Minecraft Portfolio

I've been creating in Minecraft for 8 years and have taken part of in dozens of projects, from publishing 14 maps on Java Realms to hosting community events with Yeggs, such as seasonal mapjams (72 hour mapmaking competitions with over 30 teams), managing hundred-player events and leading projects worked on by over 30 people.

Over the years, my creations have been downloaded hundreds of thousands of times (across platforms like MinecraftMaps.com, PlanetMinecraft, and others). Additionally, map trailers and showcases have garnered over a million views, with tens of millions more from YouTubers worldwide playing them.
